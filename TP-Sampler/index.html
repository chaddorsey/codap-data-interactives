The speed of sampling can be controlled, as well as whether to sample with or without replacement (in Options tab). The data generated from the Sampler will appear in a hierarchical table. The top level represents the experiment (one complete run of a simulation) and allows you to compute statistics about an entire experiment. The next level contains information about each sample and allows one to compute statistics about each sample. The items level shows each individual value drawn from the Sampler. This video tutorial explains Sampler basics.

The Sampler in CODAP was inspired by the Sampler in TinkerPlots. It was developed by the Concord Consortium as part of the ESTEEM project with support by the National Science Foundation under Grant No. DUE 1625713 awarded to North Carolina State University.
